About Us

Upholding the Integrity of the Legal Profession

Collegiality & Veritas

The Plateau Lawyers Bar Forum (PLBF) is a non-partisan, non-political association of legal practitioners called to the Nigerian Bar, practicing in or originating from Plateau State. Established to foster unity, professional excellence, and mutual support among members.

We serve as a platform for intellectual discourse, advocacy for judicial reforms, and the protection of the rights and privileges of our members while contributing meaningfully to the development of the legal profession in Nigeria.
